i3-wm
Only mentions the most important features, not everything is listed here.

Close Window
    Super + Shift + Q

Rofi search menu (d menu)
    Super + D

End session / log out
    Super + Shift + E

Move focused window to another workspace
    Super + Shift 1-9

Change workspaces
    Super + 1-9

Access sysZ helper menu
    Super + Z

Lock workstation
    Super + L

Access rofi fav gui menu
    Super + i
